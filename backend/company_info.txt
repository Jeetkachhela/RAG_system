# Kanan International (kanan.co)

**Overview**
Kanan International is one of the oldest and largest study abroad consultancies in India, established in 1996. Headquartered in Vadodara, Gujarat, the organization provides end-to-end guidance for students aiming to pursue higher education in countries such as Canada, the USA, the UK, Australia, Germany, France, Ireland, and New Zealand.

**Core Services**
Kanan International offers a comprehensive range of services to support students throughout their study abroad journey:
- **University Admissions**: Assistance with course selection, applications, and securing admissions through partnerships with hundreds of universities and colleges worldwide.
- **Test Preparation**: Coaching for standardized international exams, including IELTS, PTE, TOEFL, SAT, GRE, GMAT, and Duolingo.
- **Visa Guidance**: Expert support with visa procedures and documentation to maximize success rates.
- **Additional Support**: Help with Statement of Purpose (SOP) writing, scholarships, education loans, and pre-departure preparations.

**Company Profile**
- **Experience**: Over 25 years of experience, growing from a single small office to a large organization with a pan-India presence.
- **Mission**: To support students at every point in their study abroad journey with reliable services, products, and solutions.
- **Vision**: To be a truly global leader as a higher education partner for students, recruiters, and educational institutions.
- **Reach**: Kanan International has placed thousands of students into top international institutions globally and maintains a vast network of authorized sub-agents, partners, and franchises.
