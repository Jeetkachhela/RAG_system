# Kanan International: Leadership & Corporate Structure

Kanan International was founded in 1996 in Vadodara, Gujarat, and has since grown into a premier overseas education consultancy.

## Founders & Executive Leadership
*   **Manish Shah (Managing Director & Founder)**: A serial entrepreneur with over 25 years of experience in international student recruitment. Manish founded Kanan International in Vadodara and has been the driving force behind its expansion to over 300 team members.
*   **Sonal Shah (Director & Co-founder)**: Head of the USA Department. Sonal is a registered member of CICC (formerly ICCRC) and AIRC, specializing in USA student visas and admissions.
*   **Vatsal Juvariwala (Co-founder)**: Instrumental in the early establishment and strategic growth of the organization since 1996.
*   **Vicky Ray (Vice President)**: An alumnus of IIM Ahmedabad, Vicky serves as the Global Mobility & Visa Strategist.

## Company Legacy
- **Established**: 1996.
- **Headquarters**: Vadodara, Gujarat.
- **Service Portfolio**: Student Visas, University Admissions, IELTS/GRE/TOEFL Coaching (Kanan Prep), and Immigration Services.
- **Scale**: Representing 650+ universities and helping over 1 lakh students to date.
- **University Admissions**: Assistance with course selection, applications, and securing admissions through partnerships with hundreds of universities and colleges worldwide.
- **Test Preparation**: Coaching for standardized international exams, including IELTS, PTE, TOEFL, SAT, GRE, GMAT, and Duolingo.
- **Visa Guidance**: Expert support with visa procedures and documentation to maximize success rates.
- **Additional Support**: Help with Statement of Purpose (SOP) writing, scholarships, education loans, and pre-departure preparations.

- **Experience**: Over 25 years of experience, growing from a single small office to a large organization with a pan-India presence.
- **Mission**: To support students at every point in their study abroad journey with reliable services, products, and solutions.
- **Vision**: To be a truly global leader as a higher education partner for students, recruiters, and educational institutions.
- **Reach**: Kanan International has placed thousands of students into top international institutions globally and maintains a vast network of authorized sub-agents, partners, and franchises.
